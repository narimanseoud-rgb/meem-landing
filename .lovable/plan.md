

# Meme Media Hub - Marketing Agency Website

## Overview
A bold, cutting-edge marketing agency website that embodies internet culture with smooth scroll-based animations, a striking orange (#FF8800) accent color, and an editorial, immersive layout that feels alive with motion.

---

## Visual Design System

- **Primary Color**: Vibrant orange #FF8800 for CTAs, accents, and highlights
- **Dark Theme**: Black and dark charcoal backgrounds for premium feel
- **Typography**: Bold headlines with clean body text, high contrast
- **Motion**: Framer Motion for scroll-triggered animations throughout

---

## Sections & Features

### 1. Hero Section
- Full-screen immersive hero with animated gradient background
- Floating geometric shapes in orange for visual interest
- Bold headline: "We Turn Culture Into Growth"
- Subheadline about being a culture-first marketing agency
- Animated "Work With Us" CTA button
- Gradient overlay fading from black at bottom to transparent at top

### 2. About Us Section
- Introduction to Meme Media Hub as a culture-driven agency
- Key value propositions: internet culture expertise, meme marketing, social storytelling
- Scroll-triggered text reveals and fade-ups
- Abstract orange accent shapes with parallax motion

### 3. Services Section
- 5 service cards with animated entrances:
  - Social Media Marketing
  - Meme Marketing & Viral Content
  - Branding & Creative Direction
  - Content Strategy
  - Campaign Ideation
- Hover effects with scale and glow
- Staggered scroll animations for each card

### 4. Portfolio Section
- 6 project showcase in a responsive grid
- Each card: project thumbnail, brand name, description
- Hover effects: scale, overlay reveal, text slide-up
- Fade-in animations as cards enter viewport

### 5. Trusted By Section
- Logo showcase of companies worked with
- Clean, minimal presentation
- Staggered fade-in animations
- Subtle hover interactions

### 6. Contact Section
- Modern, dark-themed contact form
- Fields: Name, Email, Company, Message
- Focus animations on form fields
- CTA: "Let's Build Something Viral"
- Form designed for future email integration

### 7. Footer
- Minimal dark design
- Brand name and social media links
- Subtle animated orange accent line

---

## Animation & Motion Design

- Scroll-triggered animations on all sections
- Parallax effects on backgrounds and accent elements
- Staggered entrance animations for lists and grids
- Smooth easing and natural motion curves
- Subtle looping micro-animations on the hero
- Hover state animations on all interactive elements

---

## Technical Implementation

- Framer Motion for all scroll and interaction animations
- Intersection Observer for performance optimization
- Fully responsive design (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- Optimized for smooth 60fps performance
- Clean component architecture for maintainability
